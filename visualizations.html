Assignment 3 Vega-Lite Visualizations

Visualization 1: Global Sales by Genre and Platform

Action games have the most game sales, with 1751.18 million units sold.

The PS2 has the most game sales, with 1255.64 million units sold. The top 3 platforms are all home consoles.

Visualization 2: Sales Over Time by Platform and Genre

There tends to be a sharp increase in game sales on popular platforms like the PS2 during the first few years of its release. The sales peak near the middle before dropping sharply as new platforms are released.

The action and sports genres saw peak sales in 2009. In 1985, the platform genre had the largest number of sales compared to the other genres. However, its sales have been lower compared to more popular genres in recent years.

Visualization 3: Regional Sales vs. Platform

In North America, Europe, and Other regions, the XBOX 360, PS2, PS3, and Wii platforms are in the top 4 for sales. On the other hand, in Japan, games on the DS have the most sales. The Nintendo platforms also have high sales in Japan, with the SNES and NES in the top 5. However, SNES and NES have lower game sales compared to other platforms in North America, Europe, and Other regions.

Visualization 4: Number of SEGA Role-Playing Games Released per Year

The number of role-playing games SEGA published peaked in 2008, with 11 games released that year. Overall, the number of RPGs released over time is quite consistent, with around 1-4 games released per year.
